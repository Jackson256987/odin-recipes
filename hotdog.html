Hotdog

How to make a HOT dog.

Ingredients

  • Raw hotdog
  • hotdog bun
  • mustard
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Put the hotdog in a microwave for 1-2 minutes depending on how chewy you prefer your hotdogs.
  2. Put the hotdog in the bun.
  3. Top the hotdog with whichever toppings you prefer.
  4. Enjoy!
