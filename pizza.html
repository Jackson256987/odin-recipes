Pizza

This is a quick, easy recipe for when you have a pizza craving but are too broke to order one from Dominos.

Ingredients

  • 1 14.5oz can of whole peeled tamatoes, undrained
  • 1/2tsp dried oregano
  • kosher salt
  • 11lb package regrigerated pizza dough
  • 8oz fresh mozzarella
  • 8 fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees F. In a small saucepan, over medium heat, combine the tomatoes and their juices, the oregano, and 1/4tsp salt. bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer, crushing the tomatoes with a wooden spoon until the sauce thickens, 10-15 mintues.
  3. Shape the doughinto one 12-14in round and place on a baking sheet. Cover with the sauce and top with the cheese.
  4. Bake until the crust is golden brown and crisp, about 20 minutes.
  5. Transfer to a cutting board and sprinkle with the basil. Slice and divide among individual plates.
